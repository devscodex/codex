**Fix:** This usually means your Apple ID certificate needs to be trusted. Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find your Apple ID, and tap **Trust**. If the issue persists, re-sign the IPA via Sideloadly.

---

### ❌ "Injection fails on PC — no error shown"
**Fix:** Run `CodexExecutor.exe` as Administrator. Disable all antivirus software temporarily. Confirm you're injecting after fully joining a game server, not from the Roblox home screen.

---

### ❌ "Windows Defender deletes Codex on extraction"
**Fix:** Before extracting the `.zip`, add your target folder to Windows Defender's exclusion list via **Windows Security → Virus & Threat Protection → Manage Settings → Exclusions**.

---

### ❌ "'Roblox not detected' message appears"
**Fix:** Launch Roblox first and wait until you're fully inside a game world. Then switch to Codex and click Inject. Injecting from the menu screen is not supported.

---

### ❌ "Script Hub shows blank / fails to load"
**Fix:** This is a network connectivity issue. Check your internet connection and disable any VPN temporarily. Restart Codex and wait 30 seconds for the hub to sync.

---

### ❌ "Game FPS drops significantly after injection"
**Fix:** Close all background applications before launching Roblox. On mobile, free at least 1.5 GB of RAM. On PC, lower Roblox graphics settings to reduce the combined rendering load.
